Virat Kohli

About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, is regarded as one of the greatest cricketers of all time. His cricket journey began at the age of 9, and he soon emerged as a top batsman, becoming the captain of the Indian national team.

Known for his unmatched consistency and aggressive playing style, Virat has shattered countless records. Off the field, he is a proud father of two children—his daughter Vamika and son Akaay, born recently.

"Self-belief and hard work will always earn you success." - Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli's Family

Virat Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma. The couple tied the knot in December 2017 in a beautiful ceremony in Italy. Together, they are blessed with two children—a daughter, Vamika, born in January 2021, and a son, Akaay, who joined their family recently in 2024.

Virat comes from a close-knit family. His father, Prem Kohli, was a criminal lawyer, and his mother, Saroj Kohli, is a homemaker. He has an elder brother, Vikas Kohli, and an elder sister, Bhavna Kohli, who are his pillars of support.
